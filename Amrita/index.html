Hostel

Amrita provides hostel facility
for both boys and girls.
The 10 story hostel building
charges Rs. 75,000 per annum
including mess food while the other
charges Rs. 50,000 per year. Mess food is prepared in fresh
and hygienic methods, served to
students all day and night.
Four students share a room/flat
which has two bunk beds,
study tables, and two cupboards.
There are two common washrooms on every floor.
