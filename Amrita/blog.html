Our certificates and Online Courses

Amrita AHEAD’s Online Degree programs focuses on an all-rounded individualized learning trajectory to enhance and diversify one’s career growth pathways while integrating Amrita’s philosophy of ‘Education for Life and Education for Living’. Innovative and award-winning pedagogic practices provide experiential learning allowing freshers and working professionals anytime access and flexibility with personalized mentorship and interactivity. Our UGC entitled future ready degree programs are offered jointly with Industry stalwarts, reputed national and international faculty that equip and expose students to address real-life applications.
The Online Bachelor of Computer Application (BCA) at Amrita AHEAD is a 3-year undergraduate online degree course designed to equip students with necessary professional skills in the area of computer science.
It provides a sound academic base from which an advanced career in computer applications including the latest developments in the industry can be developed.
The Programme also carries out the required analysis and synthesis involved in computer systems, information systems and computer applications.
